Data Base Administrator

Minimum Job Requirements

  1. Education:  A bachelor's or higher degree in computer science.
  2. Programming Skills:  SQL, Python, Powershell, Ruby, Julia and Shell-scripting.
  3. DBMS technologies:  Proficiency in in one or more database technologies such as Oracle, MySQL, SQL Server, PostgreSQL, MongoDB.
  4. Job Experience:  As per company's requirement.
  5. Analytical skills:  Should possess strong analytical abilities to troubleshoot issues, identify patterns and solve complex problems related to databases and data management.
  6. Knowledge in Security:  Knowledge of database security best practices that includes access control, encryption, user management and compliance with regulatory standards like GDPR, HIPAA or PCI DSS.
  7. Backup and Recovery Experience:  Experience in developing backup and recovery strategies along with implementation of disaster recovery plans and ensuring data integrity and availability.

Apart from technical skills, employers will also seek candidates with strong problem-solving abilities, critical thinking, communication skills a continuous learning mindset and various others requirements besides the above mentioned.Therefore the candidates are requested to visit the company's website.Candidates tailoring the skill sets to meet the job requirements can enhance one's competitiveness in the job market.
