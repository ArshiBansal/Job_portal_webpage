Graphic Designer

Minimum Job Requirements

  1. Education:  A bachelor's degree in graphic design, visual arts or fine arts.
  2. Design Software:  Master of tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign etc.
  3. Job Experience:  As per company's requirement.
  4. Communication Skills:  Ability to understand client needs and effectively communicate design concepts.
  5. Understanding of Design Principles:  Knowledge of color theory, typography, layout design and visual heirarchy.
  6. Attention to Detail:  Precision and attention to fine details in design work.

Apart from technical skills, employers will also seek candidates with strong problem-solving abilities, critical thinking, communication skills a continuous learning mindset and various others requirements besides the above mentioned.Therefore the candidates are requested to visit the company's website.Candidates tailoring the skill sets to meet the job requirements can enhance one's competitiveness in the job market.
