Machine Learning

Minimum Job Requirements

  1. Education:  A bachelor's or higher degree in computer science.
  2. Programming Skills:  R, Python, Java, C++ and Julia.
  3. Deep Learning Expertise:   Knowledge of deep learning frameworks like TensorFlow, PyTorch etc and experience in implementing neural networks for tasks like image recognition, natural language processing etc.
  4. Job Experience:  As per company's requirement.
  5. Big Data Technologies:  Familiare with big data processing tools and technologies like Apache Spark, Hadoopetc or databases such as SQL and NoSQL.
  6. Model Evaluation and Validation:  Knowledge of techniques for evaluating model performance, cross-validation, hyperparameter tuning and avoiding overfitting or underfitting.
  7. Domain Knowledge:  Deep knowledge of the domain or industry where machine learning is be applied is crucial for developing effective models tailored to specific problems in certain companies.

Apart from technical skills, employers will also seek candidates with strong problem-solving abilities, critical thinking, communication skills a continuous learning mindset and various others requirements besides the above mentioned.Therefore the candidates are requested to visit the company's website.Candidates tailoring the skill sets to meet the job requirements can enhance one's competitiveness in the job market.
